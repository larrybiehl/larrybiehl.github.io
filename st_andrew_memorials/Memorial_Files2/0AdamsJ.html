Juanita C. Adams

March 15, 1923 to August 13, 2010

 

Relationship to St. Andrew United Methodist Church:  Juanita was a long-time member of St. Andrew United Methodist Church.  For many years she served as church treasurer.

 

Family:  Juanita was born in Martinsville, IN in 1923 to Jennie Albright Clark and Harry Clark.  She had one sister, Phyllis C. Hiner.  Juanita married Royce B. Adams in 1946.  He was a math teacher at Delphi, Battle Ground and Harrison.  He died in 1983.  Juanita was survived by two children:  Leslie Adams Cherry (husband Richard) and Clark Adams (wife Denise Holmes), as well as two granddaughters, Kaitlyn Ann and Morgan Leigh Cherry.

 

Recognitions, Activities, Special Interests: Juanita enjoyed attending church and church activities, her friends, reading, bridge, and volunteerism. 

 

Memorial:   Funds were used for construction of the Picnic Shelter on Church grounds.

 

 

Juanita C. Adams, 87

August 15, 2010

Juanita Clark Adams, 87, of West Lafayette, died
Friday, Aug. 13, 2010, at Westminster Village, West
Lafayette.

She was born March 25, 1923, in Martinsville.

In 1946 she married Royce B. Adams, a former math
teacher at Delphi, Battle Ground and Harrison High
Schools, in Martinsville. He died in 1983.

Mrs. Adams worked for many years as a legal
secretary for Obear and Overholser in Delphi.

She was a member of St. Andrew United Methodist
Church in West Lafayette. An avid bridge player and
traveler, she was a frequent community service
volunteer, especially working to support abused
women.

Surviving are a daughter, Leslie Adams Cherry of
Orlando, Fla., and a son Clark Adams of Brookfield,
Vt.

Also surviving are two granddaughters, Kaitlyn and
Morgan Cherry of Orlando.

A memorial service for Juanita will be conducted at
St. Andrew Church, 4703 N. 50 West, at 7 p.m.
Tuesday, Aug. 17. The service will be preceded by a
fellowship dinner at 5:30 p.m. and a reception at 6:
30 p.m., also at the church. Soller-Baker Funeral
Home in charge of arrangements. Leave
condolences at www.soller-baker.com
