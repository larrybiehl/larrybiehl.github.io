E. Gay Best

1879 to January 5, 1963

 

Relationship to St. Andrew:  Mrs. Best was a charter member of St. Andrew EUB Church.

 

Family:  Mrs. Best married Benjamin W. Best in 1905 and they had no children. 

 

Recognitions:  Mrs. Best was a farm wife in the Montmorenci Area and moved to West Lafayette in 1929 upon the death of her husband.  She lived in West Lafayette for thirty-four years.

 

Memorial: The Perpetual Light above the altar was purchased in memory of Mrs. Best.
