Gorman M. Brown

February 2, 1901 to May 21, 1963

 

Relationship to St. Andrew:  Mr. Brown was a charter member of St. Andrew EUB Church and served on its Administrative Board.

 

Family: Gorman Brown and Jeannette Jenners were married in 1920.  They had two daughters, Mrs. Robert Douglass and Mrs. Wallace Douglas, and four grandchildren.

 

Recognitions: Mr. Brown was a community leader as a member of the Chamber of Commerce and also was a horseman being a member of the Lafayette Saddle Club.

 

Memorial: A grand piano and a permanent plant and vase placed in the Narthex were given in honor of Mr. Brown.
