Eileen V. Compton

Sept. 24, 1922 to Aug. 15, 1999

 

Relationship to St. Andrew:  Eileen was a charter member of the St. Andrew EUB.

 

Family: Eileen was married to LeRoy Compton.  They had three sons, Alan, William and David and one daughter Jane Meade.

 

Memorial Use:  The gifts were included in the General Memorial Fund
