Karmen D. Deturk

March 15, 1987 to February 28, 1996

 

Relationship to St. Andrew:  Karmen had attended St. Andrew since moving to West Lafayette in 1993. 

 

Family: Karmen was born in Wichita Falls, Texas.  Her Parents were Philip and Diane DeTurk.  She had two sisters, Kara E. Booher and Kristen L. DeTurk.  Her grandparents were Philip and Colleen DeTurk.

 

Recognition:  Karmen was in the third grade at Klondike Elementary School were she had been active in a physical education club.  She was a Brownie also.

 

Memorial Uses:  The gifts were placed in the General Memorial Fund.  A special memorial fund was also established at Klondike Elementary School.
