Bessie E. Ellis

June 23, 1917 to May 21, 1991

 

Relationship to St. Andrew: Bessie had moved to West Lafayette to be near her son and became a Member of St. Andrew.

 

Family: Bessie married B. Gail Ellis in 1936 and he preceded her in death. They had one son, Ron Ellis, and a granddaughter, Peggy.

 

Recognitions: Bessie had been a Librarian at the Columbus, IN high school and was a skilled bridge player.

 

Memorial: Gifts were placed in the General Memorial Fund.
